Principles

A radio, not a playlist

Every MALLWAVE channel is a continuous broadcast. Nobody skips, nobody shuffles — you tune in, and the night does the rest.

  • Three channels — Plaza, Arcade FM, Rainline
  • Human-curated loops, refreshed every Friday
  • Crossfades mastered for headphones at 1 a.m.
  • No ads, no interruptions, no algorithm

Transmission three · The Mall

From crate to broadcast in four moves

Dig

Curators pull forgotten mall muzak, city pop, and slowed funk from the crates.

Slow

Every track is pitched down and washed in reverb until it fits the sunset.

Stitch

Loops are crossfaded into seamless 4-hour blocks, mastered for night headphones.

Air

New blocks go live every Friday at 10 p.m. — the plaza never closes.
