The vows

Three promises, kept in the dark

i.

Harvest at dusk

Night-blooming botanicals are cut only after sundown, when their oils are heaviest and the heat of the day has left them.

ii.

Macerate slowly

No accelerants, no shortcuts. Each blend rests in amber glass until it stops arguing with itself — usually a season.

iii.

Bottle few

Every formula is limited to nine hundred bottles. When the garden is done giving, so are we.

The factory · the atelier

The factory way

  • Synthetic accords designed for the first thirty seconds
  • Batches of a hundred thousand, blended in steel
  • Launch calendars set by holiday quarters
  • The formula changes when the price of oud does

The atelier way

  • Living botanicals composed for the ninth hour
  • Nine hundred numbered bottles, blended in glass
  • Released when the maceration says so
  • The formula changes only when the garden does
