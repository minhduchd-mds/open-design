Four working rules.

A

Show, never tell

Reviews happen in the browser and the prototype, never in a slide about a prototype.

B

One decision per meeting

Every session ends with a single committed call — logged, dated, and owned.

C

Tokens before pixels

Color, type, and spacing are decided as a system first; screens are consequences.

D

Ship the boring parts

404 pages, empty states, and loading flows get the same care as the homepage hero.
