01

The Problem

Why good signals never become conversations

Current State

Signals Go Cold in the Handoff

  • Buying intent shows up in support chats, product usage, and webinar replays — in four different tools
  • Median time from signal to first human touch: 3.4 days
  • Reps triage from a spreadsheet sorted by account size, not signal warmth
  • After 48 hours, reply rates drop by two thirds

Before

Spreadsheet Triage

  • Weekly CSV exports from four tools
  • Accounts ranked by contract size
  • One generic sequence for everyone
  • No record of which signal sparked the touch

After

Warm Routing

  • Signals stream in as they happen
  • Warmth score ranks the queue live
  • Suggested opener cites the actual signal
  • Every touch closes the loop automatically
